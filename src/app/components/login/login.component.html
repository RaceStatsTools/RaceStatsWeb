Race Stats is free and open source tools created to track your performance on RX sim. It currently supports Dirt Rally 2.0 (Rallycross mode).

Get all lap times history, compare with other pilots, create teams and competitons to race with with friends.

HOW DOES IT WORK ?

1
Create you personnal account
2
Install RaceStats Logger application
3
Activate UDP telemetry on Dirt Rally 2
4
Launch app when playing Dirt Rally 2.0 RX

That's it, Ready To Race !
